More About Me
"SHAN PUTHA" (Kaushan Gunarathne) is one of the most influential rappers in the current Sri Lankan hip-hop scene, bringing authentic storytelling from Weeraketiya to the global stage.
Rising from humble beginnings in the southern region of Sri Lanka, Shan Putha has revolutionized the local rap scene with his unique blend of traditional Sri Lankan elements and modern hip-hop. His authentic storytelling and powerful lyrics resonate with audiences across generations.

Known for hits like "Mama Enne" and "Hitha Hadaganna", Shan Putha's music addresses social issues while celebrating Sri Lankan culture. His unique style combines hard-hitting beats with melodic elements from traditional Sri Lankan music.
Beyond music, he's become a voice for the youth, using his platform to inspire and advocate for positive change in society. His journey from Weeraketiya to national recognition serves as an inspiration for aspiring artists across the country.
